GAMEPLAY

  1. This game consists of a panel with some random number balls.
  2. There you will see a 'GUESS' section.
  3. You will get a random number in that section.
  4. You have to select that number amongst the numerous number balls.
  5. You will have a timer of 1min.
  6. Try selecting as much numbers as you can.
  7. As you go on selecting numbers, you will be getting points. For each number selection, you will gain 10 points.
  8. It is just a random game where nobody wins or lose. Just for fun.

GLITCH

  1. This glitch occurs many a times, in this you will be given a common number but you won't find any number in the balls. This occurs due to the javascript program. You can simply refresh the page.
